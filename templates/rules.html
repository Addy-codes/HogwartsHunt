    • Rules:

    • Winner will be the player who finds the Treasure in least amount of time
    • You will be given a set of clues that will lead them to different locations around the game area.
    • The clues will be related to different characters, spells, or objects from the Harry Potter series.
    • At each location, you will have to solve a riddle or complete a task to earn a clue for the next location.
    • The game will be timed, and the player with the best time will win in the event of a tie.
    • The timer will start as soon as the player enters the game and will continue running until the game is completed.
    • Answering at wrong location on the map might lead to a deadend.
    • Clues would lead you to right locations.
